madpad is here

Ultra fast "document" and {code} editing from anywhere

Text editing or code editing. Why not both?

You need to access a document on another computer? No problem, we've got you covered. You want to share your work in real time? That's easy too!

Madpad takes away the hassle of sharing google docs and allows you to work collaboratively with others with simply a URL. Instead of downloading apps on your mobile phone to be able to see your documents, you can access all of your documents easily from ANYWHERE.
